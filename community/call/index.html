IIIF Community Calls

The IIIF community calls providing a forum for new and existing participants to share their work, learn about activities across the community, and discuss IIIF technology and future directions. Anyone with an interest in IIIF may join the regularly scheduled calls.

Call Details #

  • Regular Call Schedule: Every other week on Wednesdays at 12:00pm Eastern - see IIIF Community Calendar for dates
  • Communication Channels: Meeting agenda and reminders are announced on the IIIF-Discuss email list
  • Call Notes: Community Call Notes folder
  • Call Connection Information: See the IIIF Community Calendar
